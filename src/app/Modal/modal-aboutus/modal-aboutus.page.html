About Us
Tr-EAT is an initiatve by Campus Care Network (CCN) to assist our students in need. It will be held on evert Friday where only eligible TP students are allowed to redeem a meal. With this initiative, everyone can contribute to make someone else's day. A small act of kindness which can leave a huge impact on the receiving-students . Your graciousness have allowed them to get their tummies filled!
How Can I Donate?
Here are the steps to be taken
#1
Join us as a Sponsor by creating a sponsor account via 'Register' on the login page and Login
#2
Select a canteen which you wish to sponsor your meal from
#3
Select the meal and the quantity you're sponsoring. Followed by " Add to Cart"
#4
Proceed to Checkout where payment is made
